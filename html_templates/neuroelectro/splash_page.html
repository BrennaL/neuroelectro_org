NeuroElectro: better organizing information on cellular neurophysiology.

New here? Watch a short video to see what we're about.

Join our mailing list

Specifically, we seek to extract information about the electrophysiological properties (e.g. resting membrane potentials and membrane time constants) of diverse neuron types from the existing literature and place it into a centralized database.



Our goal is to facilitate the discovery of neuron-to-neuron relationships and better understand the role of diversity across neuron types.

The database and website are still in development, but we invite you to look around. We want to hear your feedback!

NeuroSynth does for fMRI and NeuroMorpho does for neuron morphology.

The site is still very much in alpha, but we invite you to look around.

Check out http://www.neuroelectro.org/ephys_prop/3 to see an interactive listing of neuronal resting membrane potential values extracted from data tables from the literature.

You can see a listing of the electrophysiology properties that we're currently indexing here: http://www.neuroelectro.org/ephys_prop/
