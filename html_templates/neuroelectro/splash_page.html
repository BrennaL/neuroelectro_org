The purpose of this site is better organize information on cellular neurophysiology.

Specifically, we seek to extract information about the electrophysiological properties (e.g. resting membrane potentials and membrane time constants) of diverse neuron types from the existing literature and place into a centralized database.



Our goal is to facilitate the discovery of neuron-to-neuron relationships and better understand the role of diversity across neuron types.

The database and website are still in development, but we invite you to look around. We want to hear your feedback!
